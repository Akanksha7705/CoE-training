Outlier is a datapoint in dataset that is distant from all observations.A data point that lies outside tthe overall distribution of the dataset.

-> Data point that falls outside of 1.5 times of an interquartile range above the 3rd quartile and below the 1st quartile.
-> Data point that falls outside of 3 standard devitions. we can use a z score falls outside of 2 standard deviation.

Reason for outlier to exist in dataset?
 -> Variability in the data.
 -> An experimental measurement error.

Impacts of having outliers in a dataset?
 -> It causes various problems during our statistical analysis.
 -> It may cause a significant impact on the mean and standard deviation.

Ways to find outliers
 -> Scatter plots
 -> Box plot
 -> Using z score
 -> Using the IQR interquartile range
